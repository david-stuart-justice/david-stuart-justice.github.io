About This Portal

Research as Exploration

This site documents working research into software architecture—the practical challenges, design trade-offs, and implementation patterns that emerge when building systems at scale. Each research area is self-contained, allowing independent exploration and updates as work deepens.

The research is organized by topic folder, each with its own index page and supporting materials. This structure supports both discovery and long-term maintainability as new research areas are added.
